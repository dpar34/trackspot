Bio

Jeffery Williams, also known as Young Thug, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and producer. He has collaborated with many veteran rappers and achieved mainstream recognition around 2014 with several singles including "Stoner" and "Danny Glover". In 2015, he released several installments to his Slime Season series which were followed up by mixtapes Slime Season 3, I'm Up, and Jeffery in 2016. Thugger's career reached an all time high and later in 2017 he released another mixtape titled Beautiful Thugger Girls.
