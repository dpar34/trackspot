Rockstar by Post Malone

Description: "Rockstar" is a song by American rapper Post Malone. It was released on September 15, 2017 by Republic Records as the first single from his upcoming second studio album Beerbongs & Bentleys (2018). The track features a guest verse from fellow American rapper 21 Savage. The track reached number one on the US Billboard Hot 100, and reached the top spot in several other countries, including Australia, Canada, Portugal, Ireland, New Zealand, Sweden, Norway, Finland, Denmark and the United Kingdom. It became both Post Malone and 21 Savage's first number-one single in all the countries mentioned.
